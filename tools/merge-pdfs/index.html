Yes. All operations, conversions, and computations on Merge PDFs are performed entirely in your local web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your data is sent to our servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

Yes. Once this page is loaded in your browser, all core functionalities of Merge PDFs are executed client-side. You can disconnect from the internet and continue using the utility without any active network connection.
