Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe and private when using Interactive PDF Viewer?

Yes. All operations, conversions, and computations on Interactive PDF Viewer are performed entirely in your local web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your data is sent to our servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

How much does Interactive PDF Viewer cost to use?

This utility is 100% free to use. There are no limits on usage, no subscriptions, no premium paywalls, and no registration required.

Can I use Interactive PDF Viewer offline?

Yes. Once this page is loaded in your browser, all core functionalities of Interactive PDF Viewer are executed client-side. You can disconnect from the internet and continue using the utility without any active network connection.
