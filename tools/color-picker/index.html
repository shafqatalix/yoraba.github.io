Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe and private when using Color Picker & Converter?

Yes. All operations, conversions, and computations on Color Picker & Converter are performed entirely in your local web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your data is sent to our servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

How much does Color Picker & Converter cost to use?

This utility is 100% free to use. There are no limits on usage, no subscriptions, no premium paywalls, and no registration required.

Can I use Color Picker & Converter offline?

Yes. Once this page is loaded in your browser, all core functionalities of Color Picker & Converter are executed client-side. You can disconnect from the internet and continue using the utility without any active network connection.
