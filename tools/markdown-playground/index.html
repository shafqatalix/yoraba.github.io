Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe and private when using Markdown Playground?

Yes. All operations, conversions, and computations on Markdown Playground are performed entirely in your local web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your data is sent to our servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

How much does Markdown Playground cost to use?

This utility is 100% free to use. There are no limits on usage, no subscriptions, no premium paywalls, and no registration required.

Can I use Markdown Playground offline?

Yes. Once this page is loaded in your browser, all core functionalities of Markdown Playground are executed client-side. You can disconnect from the internet and continue using the utility without any active network connection.
