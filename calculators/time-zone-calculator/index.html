Is my data safe and private when using Time Zone Calculator?

Yes. All operations, conversions, and computations on Time Zone Calculator are performed entirely in your local web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your data is sent to our servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

Can I use Time Zone Calculator offline?

Yes. Once this page is loaded in your browser, all core functionalities of Time Zone Calculator are executed client-side. You can disconnect from the internet and continue using the utility without any active network connection.
