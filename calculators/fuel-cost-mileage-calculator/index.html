Yes. All operations, conversions, and computations on Fuel Cost / Mileage Calculator are performed entirely in your local web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your data is sent to our servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

Can I use Fuel Cost / Mileage Calculator offline?
