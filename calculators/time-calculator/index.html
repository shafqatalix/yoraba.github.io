Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe and private when using Time Unit Calculator?

Yes. All operations, conversions, and computations on Time Unit Calculator are performed entirely in your local web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your data is sent to our servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

Can I use Time Unit Calculator offline?

Yes. Once this page is loaded in your browser, all core functionalities of Time Unit Calculator are executed client-side. You can disconnect from the internet and continue using the utility without any active network connection.
