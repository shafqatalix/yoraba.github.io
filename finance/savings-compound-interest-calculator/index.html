Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe and private when using Savings / Compound Interest Calculator?

Yes. All operations, conversions, and computations on Savings / Compound Interest Calculator are performed entirely in your local web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your data is sent to our servers, logged, or shared with third parties.

How much does Savings / Compound Interest Calculator cost to use?

This utility is 100% free to use. There are no limits on usage, no subscriptions, no premium paywalls, and no registration required.

Can I use Savings / Compound Interest Calculator offline?

Yes. Once this page is loaded in your browser, all core functionalities of Savings / Compound Interest Calculator are executed client-side. You can disconnect from the internet and continue using the utility without any active network connection.
